1. **Diagnose:** Analyze failure points in existing book projects. Why did the prose become generic? Why did a continuity error occur?

3. **Implement:** Apply the change to `_Template_Fiction_System/`.
4. **Document:** Update the Meta `CHANGELOG.md` and bump the template version.
